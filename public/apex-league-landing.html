Format

3 pairs vs 3 pairs.
Fixed team round robin.
Race to 6. No deuce.

Scheduling — how it actually works

  • Challenges run through the RNKD App — launching May 2026
  • Until then, send challenges via WhatsApp with the Liga 2 panitia
  • Opponents must respond within 48 hours
  • Match must be played within 14 days of acceptance
  • Minimum 4 challenges completed to qualify for playoffs
  • Losing still earns 1 point — showing up always beats staying home

One day. Sixteen teams. One Apex Cup.

Twelve weeks of challenges decide the Top 16. Then everything resets — one venue, one day, playoff brackets.
